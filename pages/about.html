Confidence in Every Drop

Tricia Empire was born from a single, unwavering belief: your scent tells your story before you say a word. It is your silent signature — the invisible crown that makes you unforgettable in every room you enter.

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, we set out with a mission to make luxury fragrances accessible to every woman and man who refuses to be ordinary. We believe that smelling extraordinary shouldn't come with an extraordinary price tag.

We craft each fragrance as an act of self-expression. From the first spritz to the last lingering note, every Tricia Empire perfume is designed to awaken something powerful within you — the version of yourself that walks in with presence and leaves an impression that never fades.

This is not just perfume. This is identity. This is power. This is your empire.

— Tricia
