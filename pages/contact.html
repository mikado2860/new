FAQs

Do you deliver nationwide? +
Yes! We deliver to all 36 states in Nigeria. Delivery usually takes 1–3 business days within Lagos and 3–5 days for other states.
Are all your products authentic? +
100% Yes. We source directly from verified suppliers and every product comes with original packaging. We do not sell imitations.
How long do the fragrances last? +
Our EDPs typically last 12–18 hours and EDTs last 6–10 hours. Results may vary based on skin type and application method.
Can I return a product? +
Yes. We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unopened, unused products in original packaging. Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.
Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing? +
Absolutely! Contact us on WhatsApp or send us a message above. We offer special pricing for bulk orders of 10+ units.
